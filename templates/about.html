Andrew Giessel

About me

I am a research scientist living and working in the Boston area. My wife and I live in Somerville, MA, with two cantankerous cats and a soon-to-be-born baby. I grew up on a wheat farm in western Kansas, and moved to New England for graduate school. I deeply love the beauty of the skies & plains of the midwest as well as the shores & forests of the northeast.

I'm fascinated and driven by big ideas- patterns, abstractions, computation, etc. This passion has led me to study the mammalian nervous system, but I am interested in how these concepts manifest in all aspects of our lives. The order and diversity of the natural world is a source of constant delight and inspiration.

Outside of science, I enjoy photography, contemporary art, and music of all sorts. I am an enthusiastic cyclist (road, mountain and cyclocross), and ride for the Broadway Bicycle School / Igleheart Frames Racing Team.
