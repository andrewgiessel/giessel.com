About my Research

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Neurobiology, working the the lab of Sandeep Robert Datta. Before working with Bob, I earned my doctorate in Bernardo Sabatini's lab, where I studied neuromodulation and synaptic calcium signaling in the dendrites and dendritic spines of hippocampal neurons. My long-term research goals are to use electrophysiological and imaging approaches to understanding the circuit and cellular properties of the mammalan olfactory system.

More broadly, I am interested in sensory neurophysiology, neuromodulation, computation (neural and otherwise), mathematical modelling and and data visualization. This page lists my published scientific papers.

I also intend to use this site as a venue for sharing some of my side interests, especially in data visualazation and modelling.
